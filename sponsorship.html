Are you interested in sponsoring ASU Concrete Canoe?

First of all, thank you!

Donate Money:

We are always grateful for monetary donations, as these help us buy supplies, shirts, and snacks for those who are working so hard. This past year, we bought paddles for our paddlers. Your gift will be put to good use. If you'd like to donate money, please reach out by email at sundevils.concretecanoe@gmail.com.

Donate Supplies:

We are so grateful to local businesses for partnering with our team! We are happy to receive donations in the form of used or new supplies, or discounts if you sell these supplies! Please reach out by email at sundevils.concretecanoe@gmail.com if you have supplies that you are willing to donate!

Here are just a few supplies that we can always use:

  • Aggregates
  • Cement Mix
  • Paint
  • Tools
